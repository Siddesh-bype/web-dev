About Us

Career Craftly is an AI-based student club launched under the guidance and sponsorship of Career Craftly Pvt. Ltd. Our mission is to provide hands-on workshops, training, and projects that give students practical industry exposure rather than just theory. We connect members with companies, mentors, and internships while organizing industrial projects, hackathons, and flagship events.
